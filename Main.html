Silver Star Coding Lab

The project aims to refurbish and furnish an 800 sq ft. space to serve as one of the first coding labs for the upskilling of youth in the community of Mackenzie, Linden. Linden is arguably one of the most overlooked towns in Guyana in spite of it being the second largest city in the country with a population of roughly 30,000. This lab will be a “Smart Classroom” in which students will have access to computers, the internet, and a wealth of ICT resources and mentorship

About Us

The Digital Co. Build Future

Our Mission

is to empower the youth of Linden with marketable skills in ICT for the next generation. We will achieve this by offering foundational and industrial upskilling programs which are delivered virtually in collaboration with Nexus Hub Inc.

Our Vision

We anticipate that this will impact over 300 students, aged 11 - 18 in the Linden area within the first year of its full operation. In addition, the 800 sq. ft. space has been afforded to us for the purpose of this initiative by the Linden Secondary School MC’s Educational Institute; we anticipate greater impact to be derived from the coding lab also serving as the School’s computer lab.
